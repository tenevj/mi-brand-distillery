Who We Are



MI Brand Distillery is a trusted partner for PR and communication professionals, specializing in media intelligence services. With an average of over 15 years of experience, our team delivers tailored solutions, including media monitoring, sentiment analysis, crisis monitoring, and competitor analysis. We empower agencies and organizations to measure campaign success, benchmark their reputation, and elevate their businesses. Our core value is the happiness of our team, which translates directly to client satisfaction. At MI Brand Distillery, we are committed to providing high-quality, insightful services that enable informed decisions and outstanding results in the ever-evolving media landscape.
